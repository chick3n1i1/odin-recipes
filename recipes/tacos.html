Carne Asada Tacos

Traditional Mexican Taco filled with skirt steak that has been marinated and charred to sear in the flavor

Ingredients

  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp fresh ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • 1 orange, juiced
  • 2 tbs white vinegar
  • 2 lbs of flank steak, trimmed of fat
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine jalapeno, kosher salt, and fresh ground black pepper in a mortar. Grind into a paste.
  2. Pour lime juice, orange juice, and vinegar into a glass bottle; add garlice and shake well. Place steak in a shallow bowl and pour marinade over. Cover steak with plastic wrap and refrigerate until steak absorbs flavors.
  3. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Remove steak from the marinade and place on a cutting board. Season with salt and pepper and slice against the grain into thin 2 inch strips.
  5. Grill until slightly charred and light pink in the center, about 5 minuets per side.
  6. Serve inside a small tortilla shells with lettuce, onions, pico de gallo, Oaxaca (Mexican cheese), guacamole, and a side of lime.
