Pao de Queijo (Brazilian Cheese Bread)

Pao de Queijo or Brazilian Cheese Bread is a small, baked cheese bun, a popular snack and breakfast food in Brazil.

Ingredients

  • half a cup of olive oil or butter
  • 1/3 cup of water
  • 1/3 cup of milk or soy milk
  • 2 cups of tapioca flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp minced garlic
  • 2/3 cup oof freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 beated eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  2. Combine olive oil, water, milk, and salt in a large sauce pan and place over high heat. Bring to a boil and immediatley remove from heat.
  3. Stir in tapioca flour annd garlic, stirring until smooth. Set aside too rest for 10 to 15 minuets.
  4. Stir cheese and eggs into tapioca mixture until combined; the dough will be chunky, like cottage cheese.
  5. Drop dough by 1/4 cup-size balls onnto an ungreased baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheat oven until the tops are lightly browned, 15-20 minuets
